## Our AI/ML Offerings 
### Agentic AI 
We deliver AI agent development services by designing and implementing Agentic AI solutions and AI Agents that perceive context, reason autonomously, and act across enterprise environments. These intelligent systems automate complex workflows, operate continuously, and significantly augment human productivity across security, IT, and business operations. Built using modern agent frameworks such as LangGraph, LlamaIndex, and CrewAI, our Agentic AI solutions integrate seamlessly with enterprise APIs, tools, and internal platforms, enabling secure and production-ready autonomous workflows.
[ Read more ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/ai-agents/)
### Generative AI 
Our Generative AI solutions enable enterprises to unlock new capabilities through conversational AI, intelligent chatbots, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, fine-tuned large language models, and robust guardrails. From rapid proofs of concept to enterprise-grade deployments, we build context-aware, secure, and compliant Generative AI solutions designed to integrate seamlessly with existing workflows and scale reliably across the organization.
[ Read more ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/generative-ai/)
### Machine Learning 
We deliver end-to-end machine learning and AI development services that transform business challenges into production-ready solutions. Our offerings span use-case discovery, data engineering, custom model development, and enterprise deployment, with a strong focus on reliability. We build domain-specific ML models using Supervised Learning (labeled data), Unsupervised Learning (finding hidden patterns), or Reinforcement Learning (trial and error). Validate the model for accuracy and precision and then deploy it in the application.
[ Read more ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/mlops/)
### Data Annotation 
We provide enterprise-grade data annotation and annotation services designed to power high-performance AI and machine learning models. This includes domain-specific data preprocessing, large-scale annotation services, and custom-built annotation pipelines tailored to complex use cases such as security logs, threat detection, and operational data. With quality assurance and human verification at every stage, we ensure consistently high-quality labeled datasets that improve model accuracy and reduce false positives.
[ Read more ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/data-labeling-services/)
### Model Context Protocol (MCP) 
We design scalable MCP architectures tailored to enterprise environments, ensuring seamless integration across diverse systems and tools, and write MCP clients and servers that act like a universal connector, enabling LLMs to securely access your files, databases, and APIs without the need to rewrite code for every new model or platform. This reduces integration complexity while maintaining strong governance and control, and allows AI systems to dynamically discover and interact with tools for more efficient and scalable enterprise operations.
[ Read more ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/mcp-solution/)
### Agentic Workflows 
We design and implement AI-led workflows that coordinate tasks, decisions, and system interactions across complex enterprise environments. Our Agentic Workflow solutions focus on workflow orchestration, task delegation, multi-step execution, and exception handling, enabling organizations to automate end-to-end business and operational processes. By embedding policy controls, auditability, and human approvals at critical stages, we ensure workflows remain transparent and reliable. These workflows integrate seamlessly with enterprise platforms such as ticketing systems, security tools, and internal applications driving faster execution and consistent outcomes across high-volume operations. 
[ Read more ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/workflow-automation/)
