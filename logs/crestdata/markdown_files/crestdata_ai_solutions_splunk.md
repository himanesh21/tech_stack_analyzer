##  Our Splunk Expertise  
  

####  [ Splunk Enterprise ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/splunk/#collapse-1801)
Unlock the huge potential stored in your massive datasets by efficiently extracting machine-level intelligence through Splunk Enterprise. Our experts specialize in Splunk Enterprise to help you design scalable architectures, create robust data ingestion pipelines, develop powerful dashboards, alerts, and data visualizations, and receive deep insights into your operations. Harnessing our extensive engineering expertise, enterprises can accelerate decision-making, improve data visibility, and maximize their Splunk investment.
####  [ Splunk UBA ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/splunk/#collapse-1802)
Splunk UBA (User Behavioral Analytics) helps enterprises identify insider threats and advanced security risks through behavioral analytics and anomaly detection. Crest Data provides dedicated engineering support across development, maintenance, release validation, and customer support functions. Crest actively collaborates with the product, support, and stakeholder teams across the globe to enhance platform performance, stability, and scalability, and provide deployment strategy consulting and installation and configuration guidance.
####  [ Splunk Threat Intelligence Management ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/splunk/#collapse-1803)
Unifying threat intelligence from commercial, open-source, and proprietary providers, Splunk Threat Intelligence Management enables security teams using Splunk and Splunk Enterprise Security to identify and mitigate threats in real time. As a trusted Splunk partner, Crest Data has been instrumental in developing and managing key integrations for Splunk Threat Intelligence Management. Our deep and extensive expertise in intelligence engineering, enrichment, standardization, and automation helps enterprises transform fragmented data into actionable security outcomes.
####  [ Splunk SOAR ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/splunk/#collapse-1804)
Splunk SOAR (formerly Splunk Phantom) empowers enterprises to automate and orchestrate security operations from alert triage through incident response. Crest Data specializes in managing and enhancing integrations that power the automation ecosystems. Having managed 300+ integrations and contributed to the Splunk-supported and vendor-supported connectors on the Splunk Marketplace, Crest Data’s expertise includes integration development, long-term maintenance, and certification support.
####  [ Splunk Observability Cloud  ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/splunk/#collapse-1805)
Crest Data utilizes Splunk Observability Cloud to perform real-time, cloud-native monitoring across infrastructure, applications, microservices, and Kubernetes. Specializing in distributed tracing, APM, real-time metrics monitoring, and log correlation, we help enterprises gain end-to-end visibility from code to user experience. Our engineers guarantee seamless performance tuning, anomaly detection configuration, and scalable architecture design for modern cloud ecosystems. By leveraging intelligent alerting and automated workflows, enterprises can proactively detect performance bottlenecks, expedite root cause analysis, and optimize digital experiences.
####  [ Splunk IT Service Intelligence (ITSI) ](https://www.crestdata.ai/solutions/splunk/#collapse-1806)
Crest Data leverages Splunk IT Service Intelligence to help enterprises gain comprehensive visibility into business-critical services, linking IT performance with business outcomes. We help design service-centric monitoring frameworks using KPIs, service trees, glass tables, and adaptive thresholding to deliver contextual insights. By implementing event correlation, notable event aggregation, and intelligent alerting, our experts reduce noise and improve incident response. Through proactive health scoring, predictive analytics, and performance optimization, we empower enterprises to reduce downtime, ensure SLA adherence, and strengthen overall operational resilience.
