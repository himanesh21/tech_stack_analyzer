## Our ServiceNow Services 
### Custom Workflow Development 
We design and build custom workflows on the ServiceNow platform to support unique business processes. Through custom workflow automation in ServiceNow, low-code configuration and full-stack development, we create structured workflows for requests, approvals, escalations, and orchestration. We focus on usability, maintainability, and extensibility, ensuring workflows adapt easily as organizational needs change.
### IT Service Management 
We implement and optimize ITSM processes through ServiceNow ITSM implementation services to improve service reliability and operational efficiency. This includes incident, problem, change, request, and asset workflows designed around ITIL-aligned best practices. These implementations support enterprises evaluating how to implement ServiceNow for enterprise IT while reducing resolution times and standardizing service delivery.
### Finance Workflows 
We enable ServiceNow-based workflows for finance operations covering policy-driven approvals, financial controls, task orchestration, and cross-system handoffs. By digitizing and standardizing finance-related processes, organizations gain better traceability, faster turnaround times, and improved governance. These workflows integrate seamlessly with upstream and downstream systems while maintaining auditability and control.
### Legal Workflows 
We streamline legal operations by automating request intake, review cycles, approvals, and status tracking within ServiceNow. Our solutions help legal teams manage workloads efficiently, reduce manual coordination, and improve transparency for business stakeholders. Structured workflows ensure consistency while allowing flexibility for complex legal processes and compliance requirements.
### HR Workflows 
We design employee-centric HR workflows that support onboarding, lifecycle changes, inquiries, and case management. ServiceNow HR workflows help centralize employee requests, standardize responses, and improve experience across the organization. Our implementations focus on ease of use, role-based access, and seamless handoffs between HR teams and supporting functions.
### Security Incident & Threat Intel Workflows 
We design and implement ServiceNow SecOps workflows to automate security incident intake, investigation, prioritization, and response. By integrating security tools, threat intelligence, and CMDB context, we enable faster triage and coordinated actions across security and IT teams. Automated playbooks, conditional approvals, and intelligent routing reduce manual effort, improve response times, and ensure consistent handling of security events at enterprise scale.
### Vulnerability Response Workflows 
We design and implement ServiceNow Vulnerability Response workflows to orchestrate end-to-end remediation across VR, Application Vulnerability Response, Container Vulnerability Response, Configuration Compliance, and Patch Management. These workflows identify and prioritize vulnerabilities using asset context and business impact, while continuously monitoring configurations against defined security baselines. Automated remediation, compliance enforcement, and drift tracking reduce exposure time, strengthen security posture, and minimize configuration-driven risk across the environment.
### Governance, Risk & ComplianceWorkflows 
We automate ServiceNow GRC workflows to streamline risk assessments, policy management, control testing, and compliance tracking. Our solutions replace manual, spreadsheet-driven processes with structured workflows, approvals, and evidence collection. By aligning GRC workflows with business processes and IT operations, organizations gain real-time visibility into risk posture, improve audit readiness, and maintain continuous compliance with reduced operational overhead.
### Operational Technology (OT) Workflows 
We design and implement ServiceNow workflows to support operational technology and plant environments by bringing OT assets such as PLCs, SCADA systems, and IoT devices into a structured, governed workflow model. Using CMDB and CSDM-aligned processes, we connect OT operations with incident, and change management to enable better coordination between IT, OT, and field teams. These workflows help reduce operational disruptions, improve traceability, and provide clear visibility across OT environments.
