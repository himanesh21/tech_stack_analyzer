With strong capabilities across ServiceNow and enterprise ecosystems, Crest Data enables organizations to deploy AI-driven workflow automation faster and continuously optimize business-critical workflows.

## AI-driven Workflow Automation Offerings
### Workflow Automation Consulting 
We provide workflow automation consulting to help enterprises define the right strategy, architecture, and execution roadmap. Our experts assess existing processes, identify automation opportunities, and design scalable, AI-driven workflow automation aligned to ITSM, ITOM, CMDB, and business workflows. From platform assessments and governance frameworks to integration strategy and change enablement, we guide organizations toward optimized workflows, faster adoption, and measurable operational outcomes ensuring long-term value from their workflow automation investments.
### Enterprise Workflow Platform Implementation 
We implement ServiceNow-based enterprise workflow platforms through deep configuration, custom development, and platform-level customization, tailoring workflows, data models, and user experiences to align with business processes and operational needs. Our approach starts with understanding business processes across IT, HR, and customer operations, followed by configuration aligned to platform best practices. This enables faster go-lives, reduced rework, and a foundation that supports future enhancements without disrupting existing workflows.
### Enterprise Integrations & Data Connectivity 
We design and implement robust integrations connecting ServiceNow with enterprise systems to ensure workflows move seamlessly across platforms. Our integrations span identity systems, monitoring tools, data sources, and business applications using REST, SOAP, JDBC, LDAP, and other standards. This enables unified ServiceNow-led workflows, accurate data exchange, and end-to-end automation without integration sprawl.
### Platform Optimization & Support 
We provide ongoing platform administration, optimization, and L1/L2 support to keep workflows running reliably. Our services include configuration management, performance tuning, upgrades, incident handling, and root cause analysis. With access to certified consultants, organizations can offload day-to-day platform operations while ensuring stability, continuous improvement, and alignment with evolving business needs.
